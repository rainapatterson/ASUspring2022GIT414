Frequently Asked Questions:

  • 1. Can I track my order?

  • Yes, you can! You can track it here

  • 2. What if something doesn't fit?

  • You can return anything that doesn't fit within 14 days of receival!

  • 3. How often do you update your inventory?

  • Our inventory is updated daily, as to ensure there are no mistakes!

  • 4. What kind of deals or discounts do you offer?

  • All prices are set, unless you order in bulk, then there are deals and discounts applied to the order.

  • 5. How come there is only 1 of each item available?

  • This is because we a are a re-sale brand, any clothes we find and post are individually picked and chosen for Outfitted. Sometimes there are duplicates, if this is the case and they meet our quality standards, then we will sell them.

  • 6. What if the appointment gets cancelled?

  • This is fine, we understand that life happens. If this is the case, please let us know as soon as you can, and we can reschedule.

  • 7. The appointment I wanted isn't available, what now?

  • If this is the case, we can work together to schedule a new appointment.
